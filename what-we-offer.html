Yoga Classes

  • Hot Yoga

    Hot yoga is a vigorous form of yoga performed in a very warm and humid studio. Be prepared to sweat in this class. Mondays: 5-6pm

  • Vinyasa Flow

    A Vinyasa yoga practice connects individual poses or “asanas” with deep breaths or “pranayama” in a series of flowing sequences of movement. Designed to progressively open the body, each sequence in a Vinyasa yoga class builds upon the previous, evolving into deeper, more advanced postures as the practice unfolds. Thursdays: 5-6pm

  • Classic

    This is the yoga style you know and love designed to open up your body and improve your mobility. Fridays: 12-1pm
